What is brixy.net?

Brixy.net is a platform that allows users to passively earn online while taking advantage of the collective behaviour. It uses a bias-proof value spreading system, that objectively rewards the users according to their time of entry and the number of other participants.

Why Brixy.net?

  • Usernames are fully and repeatedly encrypted to protect against planned enrolment.
  • Limitless opportunities to earn
  • Become a part of the revolution!

How it works

The value spreading system functions on specific periods of time named cycles. During a Cycle users can use the platform to join a cycle by paying the corresponding entrance fee. (see more on Cycles and their duration)

Display Mechanism:

The value spreading system functions on specific periods of time which are called Cycles. Each user can join a Cycle by paying the corresponding entrance fee and each Cycle has a display queue. Every time a new user joins the Cycle, they will get the last position in the queue, whilst the person who is currently displayed is first. The display time of the user listed first is infinite until another user joins the Cycle. When that happens, they will collect their first deposit and from that moment they will be further displayed for x minutes. These x minutes were optimally calculated using a formula that considers the traffic of the pool upon his entrance.

Tiers:

There are three different tiers you can choose from to join: bronze, silver, and gold.


Tickets:

If you do not receive any deposits by the end of a Cycle, you will receive a free ticket and priority which can be used to join any of the following Cycles of the tier you already participated with a priority in the display queue.


Randomizer:

Your chances of being displayed are always high whichever position in the display queue you possess! The system has a randomiser that picks a random user from the queue and places him right before the displayed user.


Bonus:

The more users join the Cycle, the more money collected! If a checkpoint is exceeded by the end of a Cycle and you ended up with just one deposit (so you earned less than you initially paid for entering), you will receive an extra bonus which can skyrocket your earnings! toggle for Bonus.

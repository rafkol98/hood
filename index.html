What is brixy.net?

Brixy.net is platform that allows users to passively earn online while taking advantage of the collective behaviour. It uses a bias-proof value spreading system, that objectively rewards the users according to their time of entry and the number of the other participants.

How it works

The value spreading system functions on specific periods of time named cycles. During a Cycle users can use the platform to join a cycle by paying the corresponding entrance fee. (see more on Cycles and their duration)

Display Mechanism:

Users are displayed in a Display page for a fixed amount of time.

  • Each user reserves their display-time according to the number of people that joined in the last 5 minutes. (See more on how times are calculated)
  • Each user will be displayed for infinite amount of time until he receives his first deposit. (See more on deposits here)
  • After they receive their first deposit, they remain displayed for as long as is the time they reserved upon entering.
  • With users joining the cycle, a display queue is created where everyone has a position according to the number of people that joined before them.

    How you earn with the Display Mechanism?

    Each user can join a cycle by paying the corresponding entrance fee. A portion of that entrance fee is automatically given as a deposit (specific amount of bricks) to the user displayed. (See more on amount of deposits for each tier) As long as someone is displayed, he automatically earns bricks according to the number of people that joined during his display. After the end of the Cycle he receives the amount of bricks he collected during the cycle (See more on bricks)
